# Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook

We're excited to announce the launch of our comprehensive textbook on Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics! This interactive textbook is designed to provide students and developers with a deep understanding of embodied artificial intelligence.

<!-- truncate -->

## What You'll Learn

Our textbook covers four core modules that form the foundation of modern robotics:

- **ROS 2**: The Robot Operating System for building robotic applications
- **Gazebo/Unity**: Simulation environments for testing and training
- **NVIDIA Isaac**: Advanced robotics frameworks for AI-powered robots
- **Vision-Language Actions (VLA)**: The intersection of perception, language, and action

## Interactive Learning Experience

Unlike traditional textbooks, our platform offers:

- Interactive code examples you can run directly in the browser
- Quizzes to test your understanding
- Real-world applications and case studies
- Personalized learning paths based on your background
- Support for multiple languages including Roman Urdu

## Who Is This For?

This textbook is designed for:

- University students studying robotics or AI
- Developers transitioning into robotics
- Researchers exploring embodied AI
- Engineers working on robotic systems
