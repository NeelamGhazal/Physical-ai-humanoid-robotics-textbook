# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics. This textbook covers the essential concepts and technologies in embodied artificial intelligence.

## Course Overview

This textbook is structured around four core modules:

- **ROS 2**: Robot Operating System for robotics development
- **Gazebo/Unity**: Simulation environments for robotics
- **NVIDIA Isaac**: Robotics framework for AI-powered robots
- **Vision-Language Actions (VLA)**: Integrating perception and action

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, you will understand:

- Fundamentals of robotics and embodied AI
- How to develop and simulate robotic systems
- Techniques for integrating AI with physical systems
- Practical applications of humanoid robotics

## Target Audience

This textbook is designed for:

- Students learning embodied AI
- Developers working on robotics projects
- Researchers in the field of physical AI
- Engineers interested in humanoid robotics

## How to Use This Textbook

Each module contains:

- Theoretical foundations
- Practical examples
- Exercises
- Real-world applications

We recommend progressing through the modules sequentially to build upon foundational concepts.
